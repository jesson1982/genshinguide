Floor 12 Complete Guide

Detailed walkthrough for all 3 chambers with optimal strategies

Chamber 1

3 Waves

Enemy Lineup:

  • Electro Maguu Kenki x1
  • Pyro Kairagi x2
  • Cryo Cryo Samachurl x3

Strategy:

  • Focus Maguu Kenki first - use Geo or Claymore to break shield
  • Kairagi vulnerable to Electro-Charged reactions
  • Use ranged characters for Samachurls

Chamber 2

2 Waves

Enemy Lineup:

  • Hydro Hydro Hypostasis x1
  • Dendro Jadeplume Terrorshroom x1

Strategy:

  • Hydro Hypostasis: Use Dendro/Cryo to break cores
  • Jadeplume: Apply Pyro when activated for max damage

Chamber 3

2 Waves

Enemy Lineup:

  • Geo Ruin Golem x2
  • Pyro Primo Geovishap x1

Strategy:

  • Ruin Golems: Attack head weak point when exposed
  • Primo Geovishap: Counter elemental infusion with shields
