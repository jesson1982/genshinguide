Current Cycle: June 2026 (Phase 1)

Floor 12 Complete Guide

Hard

Chamber 1

3 Waves

Enemy Lineup:

  • Electro Maguu Kenki x1
  • Pyro Kairagi: Dancing Thunder x2
  • Cryo Cryo Samachurl x3

Strategy Tips:

  • Focus on breaking Maguu Kenki's shield first using Geo or Claymore characters
  • Kairagi enemies are vulnerable to Electro-Charged reactions
  • Use ranged characters to handle Cryo Samachurls from distance

Recommended Team:

Main DPS Neuvillette / Ayaka
Sub-DPS Raiden Shogun
Support Kazuha / Zhongli
Healer Bennett / Kokomi

Chamber 2

2 Waves

Enemy Lineup:

  • Hydro Hydro Hypostasis x1
  • Dendro Jadeplume Terrorshroom x1
  • Anemo Anemo Hypostasis x1

Strategy Tips:

  • Hydro Hypostasis: Use Dendro or Cryo to break cores efficiently
  • Jadeplume: Apply Pyro when it's in activated state for maximum damage
  • Anemo Hypostasis: Hit the core during vulnerable phases, avoid Anemo attacks

Chamber 3

2 Waves

Enemy Lineup:

  • Geo Ruin Golem x2
  • Pyro Primo Geovishap x1
  • Cryo Cryo Regisvine x1

Strategy Tips:

  • Ruin Golems: Attack the weak point on head when exposed
  • Primo Geovishap: Use elemental shields to counter its infusion
  • Cryo Regisvine: Break the corolla at the base, then attack the crown

General Abyss Tips

💰 Energy Management

Prioritize Energy Recharge substats on support characters. Most bursts cost 60-80 energy, aim for 150-200% ER on supports.

⚔️ Weapon Choices

Don't sleep on 3-star weapons! Thrilling Tales and Debate Club can be excellent on support characters.

🎁 Ley Line Disorders

Always read the Ley Line Disorder before starting. Some buffs can dramatically change team viability.

⭐ Star Strategy

For 3-star clears, focus on speed over survival. Sometimes sacrificing a character for faster clear is worth it.
