Veluriyam Peak Guide

Complete walkthrough for the new Version 5.8 region

Exploration Overview

Veluriyam Peak is a new mountainous region added in Version 5.8, located in northern Sumeru. Features unique vertical gameplay mechanics.

Key Areas to Explore

  • Peak Summit: Highest point with challenging wind currents and floating platforms
  • Ancient Ruins: Underground puzzle area with mechanism-based challenges
  • Forest Valley: Dense woodland with hidden chests and wildlife

Local Legends Bosses

1

Thundering Rifthound

Elite boss at northern cliff. Drops special trophy material. Requires team with good mobility.

2

Stonehide Lawachurl

Guardian of ancient ruins. Use Geo attacks to break shield efficiently.

Chest Locations

Total chests in region: 112 (Common: 45, Exquisite: 38, Precious: 20, Luxurious: 9)

🗺 Exploration Tip:

Use Anemo characters for wind current navigation and Geo characters for rock puzzles. Bring food for stamina management.
