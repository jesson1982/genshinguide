Character Material Farming

Efficient routes for Version 5.8 new character materials

Clorinde Ascension Materials

1

Local Specialty: Lumidouce Bell

Found in Fontaine region near water bodies. Use Clorinde's skill to collect quickly. Total needed: 168 for full ascension.

2

Boss Material: Emperor of Fire and Iron

New weekly boss in Veluriyam Peak. Defeat to obtain charred flower materials. Farm on resin refresh days.

3

Talent Books: Scorching Might Series

New domain in Sumeru. Runs on Monday/Thursday/Sunday. Use Double Talent Book events when available.

💰 Resin Efficiency Tip:

Use Condensed Resin for domain runs and save Original Resin for boss fights. Co-op with friends to share boss kills.
