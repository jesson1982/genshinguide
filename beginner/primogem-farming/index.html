Primogem Farming Guide

Maximize your Primogem income with efficient daily routines

Efficient Farming Strategy

1

Complete Daily Commissions

Finish all 4 daily quests and claim bonus from Katheryne. Most reliable 80 Primogems/day.

2

Participate in All Events

Events offer best Primogem-to-time ratio. Most give 420-1000+ Primogems plus materials.

3

Clear Spiral Abyss

Even 18 stars on Floors 9-12 gives 300 Primogems twice monthly. Practice to improve!

4

Explore Thoroughly

Each region has hundreds of chests. Use interactive maps to find missed chests.

💰 Monthly Estimate:

Following this routine earns approximately 3,000-4,000 Primogems/month (18-25 wishes) without spending money!
