Getting Started Guide

Essential tips for new Travelers beginning their journey in Teyvat

First Steps as a Traveler

1. Character Priority

  • Main DPS First: Focus resources on one strong damage dealer
    • Recommended: Diluc, Keqing, Tighnari, or any 5-star DPS
    • Great 4-star options: Razor, Ningguang, Yanfei
  • Balanced Team: 1 DPS + 1 Sub-DPS + 1 Support + 1 Healer
  • Don't Spread Resources: Level main team to 60/70 first

2. Early Game Progression

  1. Follow Archon Quests to unlock features (Domains, Serenitea Pot)
  2. Explore starting region thoroughly for chests
  3. Complete Daily Commissions every day (80 Primogems)
  4. Use Resin efficiently - don't let it cap at 160
  5. Join events for free rewards

3. Resource Management

⚠️ Important Don'ts:
  • Don't use Primogems to refresh Resin
  • Don't level weapons/artifacts below 4-star past early game
  • Don't forget Battle Pass and event rewards
  • Don't waste Mora on unused characters

4. Wish Strategy

Save Primogems for Limited Character Banners only. Never spend on Standard Banner. Aim for 160 wishes (90 pity) to guarantee a 5-star.

💰 Monthly Income:

Following daily routines earns ~3,000-4,000 Primogems/month (18-25 wishes).
