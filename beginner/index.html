Getting Started

First Steps as a Traveler

Welcome to Teyvat! As a new player, here are the most important things to focus on:

1. Character Priority

  • Main DPS First: Focus resources on one strong main damage dealer
    • Recommended starters: Diluc, Keqing, Tighnari, or any 5-star DPS
    • Great 4-star options: Razor, Ningguang, Yanfei
  • Build a Balanced Team: Aim for 1 DPS + 1 Sub-DPS + 1 Support + 1 Healer
  • Don't Spread Resources: Level your main team to 60/70 before investing in others

2. Early Game Progression

  1. Follow Archon Quests to unlock features (Domains, Serenitea Pot, etc.)
  2. Explore your starting region thoroughly for chests and materials
  3. Complete Daily Commissions every day for steady Primogem income
  4. Use Resin efficiently - don't let it cap at 160
  5. Join events for free rewards and limited-time resources

3. Resource Management Tips

⚠️ Important Don'ts:
  • Don't use Primogems to refresh Resin or buy items from Paimon's shop
  • Don't level up weapons/artifacts below 4-star rarity past early game
  • Don't forget to claim Battle Pass and event rewards
  • Don't waste Mora on characters you won't use

Efficient Farming Strategy

1

Complete Daily Commissions

Always finish all 4 daily quests and claim the bonus from Katheryne. This is your most reliable source of 80 Primogems per day.

2

Participate in All Events

Events offer the best Primogem-to-time ratio. Most events give 420-1000+ Primogems plus valuable materials.

3

Clear Spiral Abyss

Even getting 18 stars on Floors 9-12 gives you 300 Primogems twice per month. Practice to improve!

4

Explore Thoroughly

Each region has hundreds of chests. Use interactive maps to find missed chests in areas you've explored.

💰 Monthly Income Estimate:

Following this routine, you can earn approximately 3,000-4,000 Primogems per month without spending money. That's enough for 18-25 wishes!

Frequently Asked Questions

Which character should I pull for?

If you're new, prioritize a strong Main DPS like Neuvillette, Hu Tao, or Ayaka. Check our Character Builds page for detailed recommendations.

Should I spend Primogems on the Standard Banner?

No! Save Primogems for Limited Character Banners. The Standard Banner is only for Intertwined Fates earned from other sources.

How do I get more Acquaint Fates?

Acquaint Fates come from leveling up Adventure Rank, completing achievements, and some events. Don't buy them with Primogems.

What's the best way to spend Resin?

Early game: Focus on Character Talent Materials and Weapon Ascension Materials. Mid-game: Farm Artifact Domains. Always use Condensed Resin when possible.

Should I join a co-op group?

Yes! Co-op lets you farm materials from other players' worlds and get help with difficult bosses. Reach AR 16 to unlock co-op.
