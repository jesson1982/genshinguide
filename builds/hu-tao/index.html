Hu Tao

The 77th Director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor

Weapon: Polearm Role: Main DPS Release: Version 1.4

🔥 Hu Tao Build Overview

Best Artifact Set

Crimson Witch of Flames
4-Piece Set

Increases Pyro DMG by 15%. Increases Overloaded, Burning, and Burgeon DMG by 40%. Vaporize and Melt DMG increased by 15%.

Best Weapon

Staff of Homa
5-Star Polearm

HP-based ATK bonus and massive CRIT DMG. Perfect synergy with Hu Tao's HP-scaling mechanics.

🛡 Best Artifact Sets

Option 1: Maximum Damage (Recommended)

4 Crimson Witch of Flames
  • ✓ 15% Pyro DMG Bonus
  • ✓ 15% Vaporize/Melt DMG Bonus
  • ✓ Maximizes Hu Tao's reaction damage

Main Stats:

  • Flower: HP (fixed)
  • Feather: ATK (fixed)
  • Sands: HP% or Elemental Mastery
  • Goblet: Pyro DMG Bonus
  • Circlet: CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG

Option 2: Alternative Setup

2 Crimson Witch + 2 Wanderer's Troupe
  • ✓ 15% Pyro DMG Bonus
  • ✓ 80 Elemental Mastery
  • ✓ Easier to farm early game

Stat Priority (Highest to Lowest)

  1. CRIT Rate: Aim for 60-70%
  2. CRIT DMG: Aim for 120-150%+
  3. HP%: Primary damage scaling stat
  4. Elemental Mastery: Boosts Vaporize damage
  5. Energy Recharge: 100-120% is sufficient

⚔ Best Weapons

S Tier (Best)

Staff of Homa (5★)

BiS weapon. Provides HP%, CRIT DMG, and ATK based on HP. Perfect synergy.

A Tier (Excellent)

Dragon's Bane (4★)

High Elemental Mastery and Elemental DMG Bonus against Hydro/Pyro affected enemies. Great for Vaporize teams.

Deathmatch (4★ Battle Pass)

CRIT Rate substat and ATK buff. Solid free-to-play option.

B Tier (Good)

Lithic Spear (4★)

High CRIT Rate and ATK buff. Requires specific team composition.

White Tassel (3★)

Surprisingly effective F2P option with CRIT Rate and Normal Attack DMG bonus.

👥 Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Classic Vaporize (Best)

Hu Tao Main DPS
Xingqiu Hydro Apply
Yelan Hydro Apply/Buff
Zhongli Shield/RES Shred

Strategy: Xingqiu and Yelan provide constant Hydro application for Vaporize reactions. Zhongli's shield prevents interruption and shreds RES. Hu Tao uses Charged Attacks during Burst for maximum damage.

Team 2: Mono Pyro

Hu Tao Main DPS
Xiangling Off-field Pyro
Kazuha Swirl/Buffer
Bennett Healer/ATK Buff

Strategy: Bennett provides healing and massive ATK buff. Kazuha swirls Pyro for additional DMG bonus. Xiangling adds off-field Pyro damage. Good when Hydro enemies are immune to Vaporize.

Team 3: Budget F2P Option

Hu Tao Main DPS
Xingqiu Hydro Apply
Sucrose EM Share/Swirl
Diona Shield/Heal

Strategy: All 4-star supports make this very accessible. Sucrose shares EM for bigger Vaporize multipliers. Diona provides shields and healing.

⭐ Constellation Value

C1

Sprint doesn't consume stamina. Quality of life improvement but not essential.

C2

Charged Attacks apply Blood Blossom effect. Significant damage increase.

C6

Hu Tao won't die when HP drops below 1 (once per 60s). Allows aggressive playstyle but C6 locks out of some events.

Recommendation: C0 is perfectly viable. C2 provides the biggest power spike. Avoid C6 if you want to participate in low-HP challenge events.

📚 Talent Priority

Elemental Skill (Spirit Soother) Priority 1 - Max First
Elemental Burst (Spirit Setter) Priority 2 - Max Second
Normal Attack (Paramita Papilio) Priority 3 - Level 6-7 is fine

🎮 Gameplay Tips

HP Management

Keep HP below 50% for maximum DMG bonus from Skill. Use a shielder or be careful with positioning.

Charged Attack Cancels

Use jump or dash cancels after Charged Attacks to maximize DPS. Practice timing for smooth combos.

Burst Timing

Use Burst at the end of Skill duration or when low on HP. Don't waste it early in the rotation.

Vaporize Setup

Always apply Hydro first, then use Hu Tao's Pyro attacks for 2x Vaporize multiplier.
