⚡ Raiden Shogun Build Overview

Best Artifact Set

Emblem of Severed Fate
4-Piece Set

Increases Elemental Burst DMG based on Energy Recharge. Perfect for Raiden's burst-focused kit.

Best Weapon

Engulfing Lightning
5-Star Polearm

BiS weapon with ER substat and ATK bonus based on ER. Synergizes perfectly with Raiden's passive.

🛡 Best Artifacts

Recommended: 4 Emblem of Severed Fate

Main Stats:

  • Sands: Energy Recharge or ATK%
  • Goblet: Electro DMG Bonus
  • Circlet: CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG

Substats Priority:

  1. Energy Recharge (aim for 250-270%)
  2. CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG
  3. ATK%
  4. Elemental Mastery
