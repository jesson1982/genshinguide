Artifact Sets Guide

Best artifact sets for every character and where to farm them

🛡 Top Artifact Sets

Crimson Witch of Flames (Pyro)

4-Piece: +15% Pyro DMG, +15% Vaporize/Melt DMG

Best For: Hu Tao, Klee, Yanfei

Farm Location: Hidden Palace of Zhou Formula (Domain)

Emblem of Severed Fate (Universal)

4-Piece: Burst DMG bonus based on ER

Best For: Raiden Shogun, Xiangling, Yelan

Farm Location: Momiji-Dyed Court (Domain)

Viridescent Venerer (Anemo)

4-Piece: +60% Swirl DMG, -40% elemental RES

Best For: Kazuha, Venti, Sucrose

Farm Location: Valley of Remembrance (Domain)

Deepwood Memories (Dendro)

4-Piece: -30% Dendro RES after hitting enemies

Best For: Nahida, Collei, Traveler (Dendro)

Farm Location: Spire of Solitary Enlightenment (Domain)

Blizzard Strayer (Cryo)

4-Piece: +30% CRIT Rate against frozen enemies

Best For: Ayaka, Ganyu (Freeze teams)

Farm Location: Peak of Vindagnyr (Domain)

🎯 Farming Tips

Use Condensed Resin

Always use Condensed Resin for artifact domains to double rewards.

Strongbox System

Convert unwanted artifact sets into desired ones at Crafting Bench.

When to Farm

Wait until AR 45+ for guaranteed 5-star drops from domains.
