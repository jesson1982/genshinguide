Weapon Tier List 2026

Best weapons ranking for every role and rarity

⚔ Main DPS Weapons

S Tier (Best)

Staff of Homa (Polearm)

Best for HP-scaling DPS like Hu Tao. CRIT DMG + HP% substat.

Mistsplitter Reforged (Sword)

Best for Ayaka. Massive Elemental DMG bonus.

Tulaytullah's Remembrance (Catalyst)

Best for Neuvillette. CRIT DMG + Charged Attack bonus.

A Tier (Excellent)

Primordial Jade Winged-Spear

Consistent ATK stacking. Great for most polearm DPS.

Wolf's Gravestone (Claymore)

ATK% substat + party-wide ATK buff.

B Tier (Good 4-Star)

Dragon's Bane

High EM + Elemental DMG bonus. Great for reaction teams.

The Bell (Claymore)

Shield + HP% substat. Good survivability.

⚔ Support/Sub-DPS Weapons

S Tier

Skyward Spine (Polearm)

ER + CRIT Rate. Perfect for Raiden Shogun.

Amenoma Kageuchi (Sword)

High ER + Energy refund passive. Best F2P ER sword.

A Tier

The Catch (Polearm)

Free from fishing. ER + Burst DMG bonus. Best F2P option.

Sacrificial Fragments (Catalyst)

High EM + energy refund. Great for Dendro supports.

💡 Quick Recommendations

F2P Players

Focus on craftable weapons: Prototype series, The Catch (fishing), and event weapons.

Whale Players

Pull for signature weapons on character banners. They offer 20-40% damage increase.

Weapon Refinement

R5 4-star weapons often outperform R1 5-star weapons. Consider refinement for F2P options.
